Tu Vo

Research Scientist at KC Machine Learning Lab (ML2)

Seoul, South Korea

I work on computer vision and deep learning. My research spans image restoration (deblurring, low-light enhancement, and HDR imaging), video understanding, and multimodal learning across audio, text, and video.

I received my M.S. from CILAB at Pukyong National University, and my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Hanoi University of Science and Technology. I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations.

Reach me at vovantu.hust@gmail.com.
